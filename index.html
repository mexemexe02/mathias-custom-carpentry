Our Services

Every cut, joint, and finish reflects our commitment to quality craftsmanship.

Custom Millwork

Bespoke mouldings, built-in shelving, wainscoting, crown moulding, and architectural woodwork tailored to your vision. Every profile is hand-selected and precision-milled for a flawless fit.

Trim & Finish

Baseboards, casings, chair rails, panel mouldings, and window/door surrounds that transform the character of any room. Clean lines, tight mitres, and a seamless finish every time.

Custom Cabinets

Kitchens, bathrooms, mudrooms, and built-in entertainment centres. We design and build custom cabinetry that maximizes storage and complements your home's unique style — from modern flat-panel to classic shaker.

Decks & Porches

Pressure-treated, composite, or cedar — we build outdoor living spaces that withstand Canadian winters and look stunning year after year. Railings, stairs, pergolas, and custom bench seating included.

Hardwood Floors

Site-finished solid hardwood, engineered plank, and luxury vinyl plank installation. Expert sanding, staining, and sealing for a heirloom-quality floor that elevates your entire home.

Renovations

Full interior renovations — kitchens, bathrooms, basements, and main-floor remodels. From demo to final trim, we co-ordinate trades and deliver a finished space that feels like new.

Built on Craftsmanship

Mathias Custom Carpentry is a family-run business based in Barrie, Ontario, serving Simcoe County and the surrounding regions. With over 15 years of hands-on experience, we pride ourselves on delivering exceptional woodworking that blends traditional joinery with modern techniques.

Every project begins with a conversation — we listen carefully to your ideas, budget, and timeline, then translate them into detailed plans and precise execution. Whether it's a single piece of custom millwork or a whole-home renovation, we treat every space with the same level of care and attention.
